The NAMASTE Way

Namaste everyone *yogi emoji*

I'd like to invite you to close your eyes (not really!) and listen to the sound of my voice, because we need to talk about email.

The word "email" probably conjures a lot of bad vibes, but it shouldn't. Email's a blessing

It feels great to get an email from someone you love. Or to learn something new from a newsletter you enjoy. You feel plugged in, connected. That’s how email used to feel all the time. It was full of good vibes, your very own portal to the universe.

But things changed.

These days, email feels like a chore rather than a joy. Something you fall behind on. Something you clear out, not cherish. Rather than delight in it, you deal with it.

What's clear is that email deserves a refresh. A cleanse. A reimagining. A revolution. An enlighenement. A rebirth. An awakening.

Which is why we're so excited to introduce NAMASTE! It’s like email, if email aligned with your chakras!

Namaste makes it simple and easy to reach “Nirvana”—achieve spiritual bliss with Inbox Zero and don’t let a build-up of old emails cloud your soul.

Say goodbye to your inbox and hello to your new “Ombox,” the mind, body, and spirit of your internet experience!

No more stress of unread messages! Instead of “mark unread,” mark to “meditate on.”
